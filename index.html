High blood sugar levels or hyperglycemia is a condition characterized by elevated levels of glucose in the blood. It is commonly associated with diabetes.

  • Hyper - excessive
  • Glycemia - glucose in the blood

You might also hear it called type 2 diabetes or diabetes mellitus. Type 2 diabetes is a chronic condition where the body becomes resistant to insulin or does not produce enough insulin to regulate blood sugar levels effectively.

As blood sugar levels increase, it can lead to various health problems. The body may struggle to utilize glucose properly, resulting in symptoms and complications associated with diabetes.
